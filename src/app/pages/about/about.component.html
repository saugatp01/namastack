Who We Are

Our Story

Namastack was founded by engineers who believed that exceptional software doesn't require proximity — it requires talent, discipline, and the right culture. We built the company around those principles from day one.

Today, we serve startups, scale-ups, and enterprises across multiple industries and continents. Our team brings depth across full-stack engineering, cloud infrastructure, data, security, and design — operating as a unified, globally distributed unit.

The name "Namastack" is a fusion of Namaste — a universal greeting of respect — and stack, representing the technology layers we master. It reflects who we are: a team that meets every client with warmth, and every challenge with technical depth.
